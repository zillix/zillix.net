Hey! Welcome to the Cameran beta!

A lot of the game is done, but there's certainly still a ways to go. Help it get there by giving us some feedback!

changelog

Things that still need to be added:
  • an expanded narrative
  • an introduction and ending sequence
  • a better tutorial
  • full art for all regions

Controls

WASD to move, Click the mouse to take a picture
Hold E and click a snapshot to delete it.
Press SPACE to delete all pictures
Press M to open/close the map

Debug Commands

Hold E when going through the first door to DELETE your save file
Hold Q when going through the first door to TELEPORT to the home screen

Objective

Explore the world, photograph the 23 creatures, and rescue the 7 lost figures

Feedback

Once you're done playing, please send us some feedback so we can improve the game! It'd be really useful to know information like:
  • what you liked the most
  • what you liked the least and how it could be improved
  • if you got lost, and where
  • if you got stuck or confused, where, and what confused you
  • what regions you explored and how far you got
  • which puzzles were too hard or too easy, and how they could be improved
  • any bugs you find, and how you found them
  • if the game ran really slowly
  • any other suggestions!
